LAW & LEGAL DATABASES
=====================

California Legislative Information
https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/
This is the official site for California Legislative information such as bills and laws.

Criminal Justice Database (ProQuest Criminal Justice)
https://search.proquest.com/criminaljusticeperiodicals/advanced?accountid=13679
The database covers criminology, corrections & law enforcement, criminal law, criminal justice, criminal rehabilitation, industrial crime, addictions (e.g., gambling), and child abuse. Coverage is from 1981 to the present.

FindLaw
https://www.findlaw.com/
This website provides legal information for consumers and legal professionals, ranging from U.S. case law, law review articles, legal news, lawyers, law schools to consumer protection. Updated often.

Gale in Context: Environmental Studies (GREENR)
https://login.libproxy.scu.edu/login?url=https://link.gale.com/apps/GRNR?u=sant38536
This database abstracts and provides the full-text of articles, newspapers, and multimedia resources related to environmental topics in science, international studies, business, law, and politics.

HeinOnline
https://login.libproxy.scu.edu/login?url=https://heinonline.org/HOL/Welcome
This database provides access to more than 90 million pages of legal information. HeinOnline includes American Enterprise Institute (AEI) publications.

Nexis Uni (LexisNexis Academic)
https://login.libproxy.scu.edu/login?url=https://www.nexisuni.com/
This database provides access to federal & state court cases, law reviews, company information, news articles, etc. Dates of coverage vary by title.

ProQuest Congressional
https://congressional.proquest.com/congressional/search/advanced/advanced?accountid=13679&groupid=98054
This database includes 1) federal bills, laws and regulations; 2) congressional publications; and 3) information about current members of Congress & congressional committees. Many publications go back to the early years of the United States.

World Legal Information Institute
https://www.worldlii.org/
WLII has databases, catalog and websearch capability into 20 countries on 6 continents. Access is independent and free. Information includes case-law, legislation, treaties, law reform reports, law journals, etc.
